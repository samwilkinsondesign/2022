Bare Biology

Bare Biology stands apart in the supplements industry with its unwavering commitment to transparency, integrity, and genuine health. Born from Melanie's personal frustration with misleading practices. Bare Biology sells only what they truly believe in and use themselves high-quality, meticulously scrutinised supplements, primarily focusing on products like pure omega-3 fish oil and marine collagen. Run by courageous women, this brand prioritises great ingredients, exceptional customer service, and well-being over corporate interests, championing women's health and laying everything bare, from ingredient sourcing and third-party test results to sustainability claims.

The brief from Bare Biology was to create a digital platform that not only drove sales but also profoundly improved their brand perception, educated customers thoroughly, and significantly enhanced the overall user experience. Crucially, the new website needed to visually reflect their unique and creative packaging, establishing a cohesive digital brand that mirrored their core values of honesty and empowerment.

As the lead designer on this project, I focused on translating Bare Biology's courageous and transparent ethos into a seamless online experience. This involved a complete redesign of the product pages, ensuring they clearly articulated benefits, ingredients, and presented genuine customer reviews. I crafted intuitive new navigation, making it simple for users to explore their range. Furthermore, I implemented specific features such as robust filtering by health benefit and diet, a user-friendly subscription model, and a 'compare products' section, all designed to empower customers with information and simplify their purchasing journey.

The result is a compelling, high-impact website that perfectly captures the spirit of the Bare Biology brand. The client was delighted with the design, which successfully transcended their creative packaging into a coherent digital brand guideline. This transformation ensures the website functions as a powerful tool for customer education and a clear reflection of Bare Biology's commitment to truth and well-being.

Dora Larson

Dora Larsen, an independently owned London-based brand established in 2016 by Georgia Larsen, has redefined lingerie by combining innovative colour combinations with contemporary shapes. Driven by a desire for comfortable yet inspiring undergarments, Georgia, alongside her husband Jake, creates two limited-quantity collections a year, encompassing both lingerie and sleepwear. A core value of the brand is responsible production, aiming to be change-makers in the industry and raising awareness for sustainable practices and our connection to the planet.

The brief from Dora Larsen was to create an elevated digital platform that not only facilitated product sales but also provided crucial information, strengthened their brand narrative, and addressed specific user experience challenges. Key among these was the need for clear sizing charts and an intuitive way to present matching underwear sets, all while showcasing their latest collections through engaging lookbooks.

As the lead designer on this project, I focused on crafting a seamless and inspiring user journey. A primary challenge was to integrate a "Complete the Look" feature, allowing customers to easily find matching underwear with bras. I also developed a custom storytelling blog section and dedicated storytelling sections on the homepage to convey Dora Larsen's unique ethos, their commitment to responsible production, and the vision of empowerment for women. Furthermore, I designed comprehensive and user-friendly size charts to ensure customers could confidently select the right products.

The result is a website that the client has embraced and actively utilises to great effect. The implemented solutions have significantly improved the user experience, particularly in helping customers get the right product through clear sizing and intuitive matching options, ultimately enhancing satisfaction and empowering the Dora Larsen brand online.

Kent Brushes

Kent Brushes, the world's oldest hairbrush manufacturer, boasts a rich heritage of meticulous craftsmanship and continuous innovation, recognised by Royal Warrants spanning nine reigns. For nearly 250 years, they have distributed their diverse range of products, from styling brushes and shaving sets to handmade toothbrushes, to over 50 countries globally, available through their ecommerce store and established department stores.

The brief from Kent Brushes was to upgrade their ecommerce platform from Magento 1, which was nearing its end-of-life, to a more flexible and agile solution. Their goal was to move away from expensive, endless development and maintenance, and instead focus resources on business growth.

As the lead designer on the project, I focused on weaving Kent Brushes unique story and British manufacturing heritage throughout the new site. Their old platform failed to communicate these crucial brand features. To address this, we created an interactive timeline showcasing key milestones in their history, engaging users and building confidence through authenticity and quality. I balanced their historic roots with their modern appeal by mixing old, black and white imagery with crisp, contemporary photography. The timeless design, subtle colour palette, and refined font combination further reinforced both the classic and contemporary aspects of the brand.

The result is a refined, high-impact platform that truly reflects the precision and energy of the Kent Brushes brand. Following the migration to Shopify, Kent Brushes experienced a 67% increase in overall revenue and a 72% rise in revenue per user. The conversion rate on their site saw a 52% boost, and average order value increased by 12%. The site also saw a 14% decrease in bounce rate and a 12% increase in average session duration, demonstrating enhanced user experience and engagement.
